A Legacy of Excellence

Patridge Bank (Patridge Bank) has been a cornerstone of Africa's financial landscape since its inception in 1961. Our journey has been marked by innovation, resilience, and a steadfast commitment to our customers' success.

From our humble beginnings to becoming a pan-African banking powerhouse, Patridge Bank's story is one of growth, transformation, and impact.

Our Core Values

At Patridge Bank, we're guided by a set of core values that define who we are:

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our interactions.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in every aspect of our operations.
  • Customer Focus: Our customers are at the center of everything we do.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation to drive positive change and growth.
  • Teamwork: We collaborate to achieve collective success.


Comprehensive Services

Patridge Bank offers a wide range of financial services to cater to the diverse needs of our customers:

  • Personal Banking: From savings accounts to loans, we're here to help you achieve your financial goals.
  • Corporate Banking: Our tailored solutions support businesses in various industries.
  • Investment Advisory: Leverage our expertise for smart investment decisions.
  • International Banking: Seamlessly conduct transactions across borders.
